Kanto Freestyle

This restaurant was a letdown. While the service was decent, the food left much to be desired. I ordered their signature dish, and it was bland and overcooked. The portion size was small for the price, making it a poor value. The ambiance was pleasant, but the noise level was quite high, making it hard to have a conversation. The dessert we ordered wasn't any better, and I expected more from a place with such a reputation. I might give it another chance, but I'm not sure I'd recommend it to a friend.
